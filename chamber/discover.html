Tresendar Manor

Come and see Tresendar Manor! The oldest sound building in the commonwealth it is more of a castle than a townhouse. Originally owned by Hilbert Tresendar, the manor is a testament to old-world style architecture. Tours can be reserved at Eldermath Orchards. There is fun for the whole family with a dinner held every Sunday at 6pm. 2sp each. 1gp for dinner.

Shrine of Luck

Come worship with us! The shrine in the middle of town is dedicated to the benevolent god of fortune and fertility. Donators need only to toss your gold into the water and ponder your misfortunes. Visit Sister Garedelle for guidance and aid.

Town Hall

Adventurers Wanted! Those gifted in violence should speak with Harbin Wester our newly elected leader for your newest quest. We are aligned with the guild of the Lord's Alliance and those with up-to-date patches will receive their guild bonuses. All applicants regardless of skill or alliance will be given a quest suitable to their ability and desire.

Eldermath Orchards

The Aldermath Orchards is the second great hub of wealth in Phandalin. Apples, Peaches, Plums, and Pears. Hiring help for the upcoming harvest season. We pay room, board, and 2gp a week. We accept married men and their families. Ask for "Carp's delivery service" in the main barn. We will set up a route of your liking at an affordable price.

Stonehill Inn

Come take a break from the rest of the world. Finest dining at an affordable 5sp a night. The most popular inn in town with a strict ban on those Redbrand Ruffians.
