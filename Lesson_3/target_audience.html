Type of people who use phandalin.com

Those who will use phandalin.com will be adventurers seeking gold or glory. They will have no job yet will be capable nd probably heavily armed. They will need something to occupy their time because, if left to their own, crime will most certainly be their first pastime.

Personas

Sildar Hallwinter

Age: 50
Race: Half-elf
Class: Fighter
Alignment: Neutral Good
Brief description:
Sildar is a retired adventurer who will take odd jobs from anyone with coin. He avoids conflict but can hold his own.

Balthazar the Gift

Age: 35
Race: Halfling
Class: Bard
Alignment: Chaotic Neutral
Brief description:
Balthazar is an incredibly prideful bard looking for places to play his loot for coin. He is always up to mischief but no crime to serious.
